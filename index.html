why exhibit art at all?

why share?


art is expression.

art is personal.

art is this.

and that.


it’s when you’re looking at random stuff on the internet, watching terrible tv, hating life on the subway, almost missing a deadline, running to pee, enjoying mere noise, smiling in a selfie, breaking up, eating meat, scrolling down, dying a few times, trying not to fall asleep, and perpetually longing for a week-long vacation that you will obviously get at one point. again.


words are art.

colors are art.

material is art.

ideas are art.

your breakfast is art.

the way you sleep in that bed is art.

nature is art.

urban is art.

human is art.

space is art and time is art.


albert einstein got it all wrong.


beautiful.

you enjoy looking at it.

something good.

am grateful for all the beautiful imagery around;

sharing with me experiences I just cannot myself.

a person —

shameless

isolation: am one, but not a whole.

is my limitation: harshest treatment i could ever impose on myself:

restrain myself from looking at all the beautiful imagery

that all the ugly people create.


all experiences are worth remembering.

memories construct space.

memories construct time.


what else do we have here anyway?



i'm asking you to share.



"we can take this huge universe, and put it inside a very tiny head. and fold it."



this is a call for entries.

this is a call to arms.


share your psyche in any color you like


this is a non-arbitrary sample of humanity, collectively defining space and time. in a room. for a night.

this is an index of individual psyches, converging to the whole, which it will never become.

this is a clumsy exposition of the human experience in infinite dimensions.


istanbul, july 2016
